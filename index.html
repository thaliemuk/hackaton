POLICY UPDATE
Starting January 1st, we now offer a new insurance policy: Electric Scooter. The Dutch law (CVC) 407.5(a) defines a motorized scooter as any two-wheeled device that has handlebars, has a floorboard that is designed to be stood upon when riding, and is powered by an electric motor. CVC 21235 mandates that all scooter riders in all of Holland must:
  1. Wear a properly fitted bicycle helmet
  2. Have a valid driver license
  3. Ride on the road and remain off sidewalks
  4. May not park scooters on a sidewalk in a position that blocks pedestrian paths
The above is a mandatory term in order for the new policy (VDH) 118.7 to take under consideration
